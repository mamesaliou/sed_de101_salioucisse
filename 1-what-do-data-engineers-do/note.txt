Steps to Complete the Assignment:

1. Understand business objectives: Identify overall goals and gather information from stakeholders to understand specific needs.

2. Gather requirements: Ask key questions about data needs, types, frequency, success criteria, and legal constraints.

3. Evaluate infrastructure: Assess current technology, scalability, and data analytics tools in use.

4. Design solution architecture: Plan for data storage, extraction (ETL pipelines), analysis, visualization, and automation.

5. Implementation and testing: Deploy and test the solution, ensuring it meets initial requirements and maintains data quality.

6. Documentation and training: Document the process and conduct training sessions for users and stakeholders.

7. Share results: Create a GitHub repository, structure it, add notes, code, and documentation, and share the link with stakeholders.
